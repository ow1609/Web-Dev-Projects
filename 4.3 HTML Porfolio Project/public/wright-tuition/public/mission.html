At Wright Tuition, I am passionate about delivering high quality, accessible and inclusive education. This is why I offer significant discounts for students who experience socio-economic challenges as I believe economic constraints should not limit how far you can progress in your education.

Discounts for disadvantaged students

I offer discounted rates for students whose socio-economic background puts them at a disadvantage. A discount is offered on a case-by-case basis and upon proof of socio-economic circumstances and cannot be back-dated or refunded from previous lessons but will be applied for all future lesson bookings where the disadvantage persists.

Here is a non-exhaustive list of example learners who may be eligible to discounted lessons:

  • Students who are in receipt of state benefits in their country of residence
  • Students who come from an ethnic minority background
  • Children who attend a UK government-funded school and are eligible to free school meals beyond Year 2 (even if the child is not yet beyond Year 2)
  • Refugees and asylum seekers

I exercise the utmost discretion when awarding discounted rates and discounted rates vary from case to case.

As part of a the discount policy, it may be that you are only able to attend small group lessons and may not be entitled to 1-2-1 tuition. However, please note that, for certain learning goals and circumstances, small group classes can provide a more enriching learning experience than 1-2-1 tuition.
