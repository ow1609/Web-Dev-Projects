Discounts for Disadvantaged Students

Boosting confidence and boosting grades!

At Wright Tuition, we are passionate about delivering high quality, accessible and inclusive education. This is why discounted rates are available for students who experience certain socio-economic challenges.

Example cases where students may be eligible for discounted lessons:

  • People in receipt of certain state benefits e.g. Universal Credit
  • Children who attend a UK government-funded school and are eligible to free school meals beyond Year 2 (including if the child is not yet beyond Year 2)
  • Refugees and asylum seekers
  • Homeless individuals
  • Young carers
  • Ex-offenders

Discounts are awarded on a case-by-case basis and the percentage discount varies depending on the circumstances. Proof of socio-economic circumstances is required and strict confidentiality is followed. Discounts cannot be back-dated or refunded from previous lessons but will be applied for all future lesson bookings where the socio-economic disadvantage persists. Please indicate if you think you may qualify for a discount during your initial consultation or first lesson booking.
